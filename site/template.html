03

戰爭從未突然爆發War Never Breaks Out Suddenly

把烏俄戰爭前的新聞剪下來、依日期排列，你會發現：戰爭早就在大家每天滑過的新聞裡，一步一步逼近。紅圈是我們圈起來的日期——每一則都是公開報導。 Clip the pre-war headlines, line them up by date, and you'll see it: the war was approaching step by step, inside the news everyone scrolled past. Every red-circled date is public reporting.

04

如何自己判讀How to Read the Signs Yourself

你不需要內線消息。一般人光是正確分析公開情報，就足以救自己跟家人一命。 You don't need inside information. Correctly reading public sources is enough to save your family's life.

一

看行動，不看言詞Watch actions, not words

言詞威脅是常態；調動兵力、儲備血漿、燒錢的事才是訊號。Threats are routine; moving troops and staging blood — things that cost money — are signals.

二

眷屬撤離是最強訊號Dependents leaving is the strongest signal

政府可以虛張聲勢，但不會拿自己外交官的家人冒險。Governments bluff — but never with their own diplomats' families.

三

要求多個獨立來源Demand independent sources

單一媒體會錯；美、歐、日官方動作同向，才算確認。One outlet can be wrong; US, European, and Japanese officials moving the same way is confirmation.

四

為每則新聞標上日期Date every headline

單則新聞是雜訊，排上時間軸才看得見「加速度」。A single headline is noise; on a timeline you can see acceleration.

05

分級行動指南Preparedness, Level by Level

每一級只要求「當下值得做」的事——太早做浪費，太晚做危險。 Each level asks only what's worth doing now — too early is wasteful, too late is dangerous.

第 1 級Level 1

把這個網站加入書籤，跟家人約好「如果升級，我們用哪個群組聯絡」。Bookmark this page; agree with family which group chat is the emergency channel.

第 2 級Level 2

護照效期檢查、證件影本雲端備份、兩週份常用藥、一筆隨時可動用的現金。Check passport validity; back up documents to the cloud; two weeks of medication; accessible cash.

第 3 級Level 3

打包避難包（水、糧、藥、電源、收音機）、確認離境航班與親友住處選項、下載離線地圖。Pack the go-bag (water, food, meds, power, radio); confirm flight and relative-stay options; offline maps.

第 4 級Level 4

可離境者離境；留下者移往遠離軍事目標的住處，加購糧水與燃料。Those who can leave, leave; those who stay relocate away from military targets and stock up.

第 5 級Level 5

立即執行計畫。烏克蘭的教訓：等到「確定」時，機場已經關了。Execute now. Ukraine's lesson: by the time it was "certain," the airport was closed.

06

本站不能告訴你的事What This Site Cannot Tell You

  • 公開情報有延遲——燈號反映的是「已被報導的世界」，不是即時戰場。Public information lags — the signal reflects the reported world, not real time.
  • 台海未必重演烏克蘭模式：渡海作戰可能更依賴突襲，預警期可能更短。The strait may not follow the Ukraine script: an amphibious war may lean on surprise, with less warning.
  • 威懾表演可能觸發指標卻不開戰——燈號會有「誤報」，寧可誤報，不可漏報。Deterrence theater can trip indicators without war — false alarms will happen; we prefer them to silence.
  • 本站非官方、非預測、非投資建議。它只做一件事：幫你把公開訊號排好，讓你自己判斷。Not official, not a prediction, not advice. It does one thing: arrange the public signals so you can judge for yourself.
